Ramen

Description

Ramen is a bowl of warm broth with noodles garnished with vegetables and proteins. This recipe has the ramen noodles and broth to be used in a pressure cooker. The dish contains: eggs, ramen noodles, egg, green onions, seaweed, pork and seasoned broth. The overall time required for this meal equates to 2 hours and 15 minutes.

Ramen can also be made without broth or even without meat and substitute with more vegetables and soy products. Ramen was first produced in Japan and now recreated worldwide. The broth can be bought in store in cartons or packages of powder.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup dried scallops
  • 1 cup dried anchovies
  • 1/4 cup dried shrimp
  • 1/4 cup bonito flakes
  • 1 piece of ginger, thinly slices, divided
  • 1 piece dashi kombu (dried kelp)
  • 3 quarts water
