Pho

Description

Pho is one of the most well-known comfort food in Vietnam. It is a meal that is both delicious and healthy with its clear broth and rich proteins. Pho is usually made with beef or chicken, but nowadays different proteins are added such as fish or pork.

Vietnamese who eat pho usually add extra garnish or sauces. Such garnishes includes: string beans, Vietnamese mint, and lemon. Sauces that could be added are sriracha, sweet soy sauce and more.

Ingredients

Broth

  • 5 pounds beef soup bones
  • 1 tablespoon salt, divided
  • 2 gallons of water
  • 2 medium onions, quartered
  • 1 (4 inch) piece fresh ginger root
  • 2 pounds beef oxtail
  • 1 white (daikon) radish, sliced
  • 2 ounces whole star anise pods
  • 1/2 (3 inch) cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • salt to taste

Other

  • 1 1/2 pounds of dried flat rice noodles
  • 1/2 pound frozen beef sirloin
